Paris

Paris is the capital city of France. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 2 million inhabitants.

Paris is a major railway, highway, and air-transport hub served by two international airports: Paris–Charles de Gaulle (the second-busiest airport in Europe) and Paris–Orly.[9][10] Opened in 1900, the city's subway system, the Paris Métro, serves 5.23 million passengers daily;[11] it is the second-busiest metro system in Europe after the Moscow Metro.

London

London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Standing on the River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.
